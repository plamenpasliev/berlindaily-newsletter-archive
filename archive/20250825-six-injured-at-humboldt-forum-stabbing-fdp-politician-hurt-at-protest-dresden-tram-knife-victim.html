🇩🇪 Six injured in stabbing at the Humboldt Forum

Six people were injured, one critically, in a knife attack near the Humboldt Forum in Berlin-Mitte on Saturday evening. Police say two groups of about eight–ten people clashed at the Karl-Liebknecht Bridge; victims (aged around 20–26) were hospitalized with stab and cut wounds. Investigations are ongoing into motives and whether the groups met earlier at Alexanderplatz; some participants may have fled. (rbb24, 2 minute read)

🇩🇪 FDP politician harassed and injured at pro-Palestine demonstration

FDP politician Karoline Preisler was assaulted and suffered a hand injury at a pro‑Palestinian demonstration at Checkpoint Charlie in Berlin on Saturday evening when two people tried to seize her placard and an Israeli flag; police detained a man and a woman and are investigating attempted theft and assault. About 250 people attended and the protest was largely peaceful. Preisler, who campaigns for Israeli hostages, said she was followed afterward; Jewish leaders warned of a threatening climate. (rbb24, 2 minute read)

🇩🇪 Knife attack in Dresden: Now the victim is speaking out

A 21‑year‑old US citizen was injured in the face in a Dresden tram after intervening when two men allegedly harassed women; one attacker, a 21‑year‑old Syrian, was detained and is being investigated for dangerous bodily harm while the other fled. The victim posted a video accusing the assailant of prior knife threats and illegal activity—claims not yet confirmed. Police note rising knife attacks in Germany (about 29,000 recorded in 2024). (Berliner Zeitung, 2 minute read)
